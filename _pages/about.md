I'm a final year PhD candidate at Inria in Scool team (previously SequeL) under the supervision of [Debabrota Basu](https://debabrota-basu.github.io/) and [Philippe Preux](https://philippe-preux.github.io/). Prior to  my PhD, I was a visiting research student at MILA, supervised by [Yoshua Bengio](https://yoshuabengio.org/), [Loubna Benabbou](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=S8bzEmUAAAAJ&hl=en) and [Dianbo Liu](http://www.cogai4sci.com/).

My research investigates the theoretical foundations of machine learning, which underpin the success of modern AI systems. My current work focuses on developing frameworks that enable policymakers to detect and assess algorithmic bias in realistic settings, particularly in dynamic and competitive markets where companies continuously adapt their systems throughout the audit process. In parallel, I study fundamental questions in learning theory  including compression schemes, and more recently, I have been working on large language models.
